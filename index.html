About

Обо мне

Andrey finished his MFA in Cinematography at Saint Petersburg State University of Cinema and Television and also gained a UCLA Extension Certificate in Entertainment Studies during his life in LA.

He finished one feature, 15 short films, and 7 music videos as a Director of Photography, and also has extensive camera operator experience. Andrey worked with acclaimed Russian directors, such as Alexei Balabanov, Andrey Konchalovsky, and Alexei German. The films he worked on were nominated on Oscar and won major European and Russian movie festivals.

Andrey works as a Director of Photography or Cameraman depending on a project.
